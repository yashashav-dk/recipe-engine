Chicken Tikka

tested Indian protein high-proteinmeal-prepgluten-free ⏱ 15m prep 🍳 25m cook 💰 $1.50/srv 🍽 8 servings instant-pot

Ingredients

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken thighs with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt for at least 30 mins
  2. Set Instant Pot to sauté mode, add oil
  3. Sear chicken pieces in batches until browned on both sides
  4. Add 50ml water, close lid, set to pressure cook high for 8 mins
  5. Natural release for 5 mins, then quick release
  6. Optional - air-fry pieces at 200°C for 5 mins for extra char

Storage

Fridge: 5 days

Freezer: 30 days

Reheating: Microwave 2-3 mins or air-fry at 190°C for 5 mins

Notes

Scales well to 2x. Use pot-in-pot method to cook rice simultaneously. Next time try adding kasuri methi.
